# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of this project, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, posting feature requests, updating documentation, submitting pull requests, and other activities.

Be friendly and welcoming
Be patient and assume good intent
Be respectful when we disagree

Reporting and enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers. This project follows the Contributor Covenant: https://www.contributor-covenant.org

For full text, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/
